My Story

Drawing on decades of experience to give spaces new life, Simone’s timeless approach to interior design is one of relaxed comfort and understated elegance.

“My interior design style is ever evolving, offering each client a bespoke and collaborative experience to create spaces reflective of their style and personality.”

We work on high-end Residential projects and deliver in-depth expertise for all phases of the design process, from initial conception to final execution. Through each step of the design process, Simone is committed to an absolute focus on white-glove service.

Simone Katherine Interior Design Studio prides itself on creating an immersive experience that’s true to the vision and standards of the clients.

We'll transform your house into a functional beautiful space with furniture, fabrics, accessories and provide you with custom pieces tailor-made for your living space.
